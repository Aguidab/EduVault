About Us

EduVault is a platform built to simplify the financial aid journey for students. Created by Aguida Bangoura, a fourth-year Computer Science student at American University, our mission is to make education accessible and equitable for all.

With a focus on user-friendly tools and personalized scholarship matching, EduVault is here to transform how students discover and secure financial aid opportunities. In the future, EduVault aims to integrate with EagleService, enabling personalized scholarship recommendations based on students' financial debt data and providing a tailored approach to reducing financial burdens.
