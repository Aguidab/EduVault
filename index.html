About Us

EduVault is dedicated to helping students reduce financial stress by connecting them to funding opportunities they might otherwise miss. My name is Aguida Bangoura, and I am a fourth-year Computer Science student at American University. I created EduVault to change the way university students access financial aid. Driven by a passion for equitable education, I want to ensure that students, regardless of their background, have the tools to navigate the overwhelming landscape of scholarships and financial support. EduVault is not just a platform but a mission to empower students by making education accessible and affordable.
