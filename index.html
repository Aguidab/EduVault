About Us

EduVault is a platform built to simplify the financial aid journey for students. Created by Aguida Bangoura, a fourth-year Computer Science student at American University, our mission is to make education accessible and equitable for all.

With a focus on user-friendly tools and personalized scholarship matching, EduVault is here to transform how students discover and secure financial aid opportunities. Join us in creating a future where every student has the resources to succeed.
